# SPOTLIGHT: The Albums Defining Late 2025

A curated selection of the records currently living rent-free in everyone’s headphones.

### 1. The xx – xx (Deluxe)
![The xx – xx (Deluxe)](https://i.scdn.co/image/ab67616d0000b2738b7b1c4b1a0e4a3b2e2e2f0d)

![Danko Jones – Leo Rising](https://i.scdn.co/image/ab67616d0000b2739e4f5a2b3c4d5e6f7a8b9c0d)

![Twin Shadow – Caer](https://i.scdn.co/image/ab67616d0000b273e8f8g8h8i8j8k8l8m8n8o8p8)

Save these. Your future self will thank you.
